About DMMA Group

A Local Partner for
Every Stage of the Build

DMMA Group Limited is a Dar es Salaam–based construction and supply company operating from Plot No. 39, House No. 6, Mahakamani Road, Ilala. We work alongside contractors, developers, mining operators and institutions across Tanzania, combining hands-on construction capability with a dependable supply chain.

Our approach is straightforward: understand the site, source what it needs, and deliver work that holds up long after handover. Whether it's a building going up or a quarry needing consistent logistics support, we treat every job with the same discipline.

  • Locally based team with direct knowledge of Dar es Salaam and regional sites
  • Integrated offering — construction, materials, mining support and general supply under one roof
  • Straightforward communication from quotation through to project close-out
